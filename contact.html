Booking Information

Booking Confirmation

After reserving your spot, you will receive a confirmation email at the address provided.If you do not receive a confirmation, your booking may not have been completed or the email address may be incorrect.
Please contact us if you have any questions regarding your reservation.



Group Bookings

For groups of more than 5 guests, we kindly ask you to contact us at info@20grams.dk so we can assist with arranging your booking and the details.


Allergies

We offer gluten-free ingredients for our breakfast and brunch dishes.


Please note that our kitchen handles gluten, nuts, and other allergens daily. While we take care in preparation, we cannot guarantee that our food is completely free from cross-contamination or traces of allergens.


As gluten-free options are not requested frequently, we kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice.


Please include any allergies (e.g., gluten, lactose, etc.) in the Occasion/Request field when making your booking. If you have multiple or less common allergies, we kindly ask you to contact us at info@20grams.dk before booking, so we can confirm whether we are able to accommodate your needs.


Contact Us

For general questions or urgent inquiries, please contact us via email or send us a message through the contact form. We'll respond as soon as possible.
