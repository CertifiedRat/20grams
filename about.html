ABOUT US

Welcome to 20 Grams, where coffee is stronger than our Danish winter jackets, and where every cup comes with a side of vintage charm!

We, Daniel, Claudia, and our son Áron moved to Denmark with nothing but big dreams, a love for people, animals, and a talent for hunting down the best thrift store finds. Every cup of coffee you enjoy here has a history than most of us. From the tables to the chairs, we have lovingly rescued all our furniture from second-hand shops—because who says good vibes have to come new?

"My inspiration comes from everywhere. I'm always noticing things that inspire me. Mostly from movies, especially old ones that I love to watch, but also from museums and art galleries. Most of all, I get inspiration from places that have nothing to do with restaurants or coffe shops, because the idea is to look where others usually don't." - Owner
