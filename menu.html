MENU

At 20 Grams, breakfast, brunch, desserts, and coffee come together for the ultimate good-vibes experience. From 09:00–15:00 every day, we serve brunch and breakfast worth waking up for—whether you’re craving a hearty feast, fluffy waffles, homemade sourdough sandwiches, or something sweet on the side. Vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options? Absolutely. Everyone’s invited to the table. Our sourdough bread comes fresh from artisan bakery Albatross og Venner, stacked with delicious fillings and toasted to perfection. And for dessert lovers, there’s Claudia’s homemade Basque cheesecake—smooth, creamy, gluten-free, and available by the slice to enjoy on the go. To drink? We’ve got everything from bold coffee and creamy matcha lattes to chai, hot chocolate, fresh juices, smoothies, and seasonal favorites like pumpkin spice and non-alcoholic gløgg.

The Menu

Whether you’re stopping by for brunch, cake, or just a cozy drink, 20 Grams is all about good food, good coffee, and good vibes. So take your time and flip through the menu and see what we have to offer.
